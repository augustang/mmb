the moments

all but not....

this body of work focuses on the patterns in life whether geometric or organic; and how these patterns merge so that the boundaries between the two no longer have any distinctions. these intimate moments become a depiction of new beginnings often representing both the vastly larger whole and the infinitesimal microscopic moments of our universe. as the layers continually form and deteriorate patterns within emerge and reveal the indeterminate nature of all things.
